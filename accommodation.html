Accommodation in

Waterford City .

Housing is a tricky situation, let's make that a bit easier shall we? There are many routes you can chose in order to get accommodation in Waterford City. Housing is Waterford is among the most affordable in Ireland, but with that, it can get snatched up pretty quick. We are going to give you the "insider scoop" on where to look for accommodation.

What Should I Look Out For?

Make sure you always try to view the accommodation before making any descisions, get down on your hands and knees and inspect the place, you will be glad you did. Don't let the landlord rush you into making a descisions. Bring your parents along! They may be able to ask the questions you don't. Below will be a selection of links on where to find housing.

Daft.ie

Bit of a no-brainer, but student housing can be found on Daft.ie

Student Pad

A website where private landlords can rent out accommodation specifically for students.

On-Campus

SETU Waterford also has it's own on campus accommodation.
