Electronic Engineering

Electronic Engineering is a course that is all about the future of technology. You'll learn about the latest developments in areas like artificial intelligence, blockchain, and the Internet of Things. You'll be taught by experts in their field and have the opportunity to specialise in a particular area that you're interested in. And the best part? You'll be doing it in the heart of SETU Waterford, one of the most innovative cities in Europe. The courses are designed to give you a truly hands-on experience of what it's like to work in the industry and we're confident that you'll love it here.

Electronic Engineering

Electronic Engineering, is an established but ever growing and advancing field. In SETU Waterford, You will be on the bleading edge of the field.
